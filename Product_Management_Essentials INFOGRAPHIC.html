The PM's Skill Toolkit

Cracking the PM Interview

Interviews mein in topics par sabse zyada focus hota hai.

PM interviews aapki problem-solving, creativity, aur analytical soch ko parkhte hain. Sabse zyada dhyaan 'Product Sense' par diya jaata hai, jismein aapse naye products design karne ya maujooda products ko behtar banane ke liye kaha jaata hai.
